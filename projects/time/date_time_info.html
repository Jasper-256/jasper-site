Date & Time Info

The first number shown here is the year from the Gregorian calendar after the start of the Common Era (CE).

The second number is the ordinal date minus 1 so it starts at 000 on the first day of the year and goes to 364 on regular years, or 365 on leap years.

Normally ordinal dates start at 001 for the first day of the year, but it is nice to use a system that always counts from 0.

After the space there is the standard 24 hour time in your local time zone taken from the system time on your device.
